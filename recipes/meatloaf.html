Meatloaf

In case you are trying to go back to the main page

Now if you are here for the famous meatloaf, you are in the right place. I have been making this meatloaf for generations. You may be tired of reading and ready to cook, So lets get talking about them ingredients.

    Materials

  • 2 large eggs
  • 2/3 cups of milk
  • 2 teaspoons of salt
  • 1/4 teaspoons of black pepper
  • 3 slices of bread
  • 1 cup of shredded cheese
  • 1 1/2lbs of ground beef
  • 1 onion
  • 1/2 cup of shredded carrots
  • 1/4 cup of brown sugar
  • 1/4 cup of ketchup
  • 1 tablespoon of prepared yellow mustard

Now that we know what things we need, Lets figure out how to make em

    Procedure

  1. Preheat the oven while gathering ingredients
  2. Whisk milk, eggs, salt, pepper and stir until dissolved. While mixing add ground beef,onion, cheese, carrot and bread mix
  3. Transfer mixture to a 9x5inch loaf pan
  4. Combine brown sugar, ketchup, and mustard into a small bowl. Then spread over the meat mixture
  5. Bake in the oven till no pink in center, Temp should read 160F(70C) in about 60-75 min
  6. Enjoy
