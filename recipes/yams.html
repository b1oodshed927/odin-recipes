Now to figure out whats needed to make this wonderful dish

    The stuff needed for the magic

  • Sweet potatoes
  • butter (preferred if unsalted)
  • Granulate sugar
  • Heavy whipping cream
  • Cinnamon
  • Nutmeg
  • Clove
  • Real maple
  • Vanilla extract

Now you may need to go to the store for a couple of other small things but don't fret you can always improvise

    How to cook it?

  1. Prep your potatoes. Wash, dry, peel and slice into 1/2 inch circles
  2. Make the glaze by melting butter and sugar
  3. Pour the glaze over the sweet potatoes then back coved for 40 min then uncover them to bake for 25 min or till they are fork tender
  4. Get a smooth spoon and serve you're delight
