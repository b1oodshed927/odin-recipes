Golden egg? No Golden Chicken

To make golden chicken you must have a golden egg. My grandma used to always say that but I never knew what it meant. Here I am going to show you what she meant. Now for this chicken to taste good you must follow everything I say. Now to go back to the main page click here

    Important tools needed for the job

  • 4lbs of chicken
  • 1 cup of butermilk
  • 2 cups of all purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon of paprika
  • salt and pepper
  • 2 qt vegetable oil

Now to get starting in cooking make sure your areas clear and ready to start frying. Remember water + oil = bad times

    Guide

  1. Cut up the chicken into the size of choice
  2. Season a full bag of flour
  3. Dip chicken in buttermilk then flour shaking excess
  4. Place chicken on tray till flour is a paste like consistency
  5. Place oil into skillet and heat up
  6. Fill pot with as much chicken as possible browning both sides
  7. Once browned reduce heat for 30 min then raise heat until chicken is crispy
  8. Drain chicken on paper towel
  9. Enjoy you're cooked meal
