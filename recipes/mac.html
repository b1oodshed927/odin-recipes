Mac and Cheese

Hello welcome to the best melt in the mouth mac and cheese you will ever get to eat. Hell people have died trying to get this recipe I am just handing out. Just look at how tasty this looks.

Don't that look just mouth-watering, Now lets see what you need. In case you need the main page click here

    Ingredients

  • Macaroni Elbow box
  • 2 brick cheese of you're choice
  • 2 cups of milk
  • Seasonings(Personal choice, However I can recommend Adobo, Season salt, and Oregno)

Now get that apron ready it's time to get cooking

    Hungry? Time to fix that

  1. Preheat the oven to 450
  2. Boil half the box of elbows
  4. Once Macaroni is soft drain water fully
  5. Bring pot to a meduim heat and stir in all ingredients
  6. Once everything is mixed in completely put in oven safe dish and let it sit in the oven for 15-20 mins
  7. Take out and enjoy
